3. Zilla Parishad Annual Administration Reports

Panchayat Raj and its Establishment

Panchayat Raj, an innovative experiment in shaping modern India, was recommended by the Balwantrai Mehta Committee. The Government of Maharashtra enacted the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Committees Act, 1961, establishing a three-tier Panchayat Raj system.

The Satara District Council, guided by Yashwantraoji Chavan, was established in May 1962. Abasaheb Parlekar served as its first president. Presently, 11 Panchayat Samitis and 1501 Gram Panchayats are dedicated to rural development.

Empowerment and Evolution

Through the 73rd Constitutional Amendment, women were granted participation in governance, and Gram Sabhas received enhanced powers. Satara Zilla Parishad remains at the forefront of implementing governmental schemes and campaigns for the betterment of rural areas.
